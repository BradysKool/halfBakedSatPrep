Area of a Triangle Using (1/2)ab sin(C)

One way to find the area of a triangle is using two sides, a and b, and the included angle in between them c The formula is:

[a * b * sin(C)] /2

In this formula, a and b are the lengths of two sides, and C is the angle between those two sides.
